# Frangipane

## Ingredients
- 8 tablespoons (4 oz; 115 grams) unsalted butter softened
- ½ cup + 1 tablespoon (115 g) granulated sugar
- 1 cup (115 g) almond flour
- 3 large whole eggs room temperature
- 1 tablespoon (15 g) unbleached all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
- ½ teaspoon lemon zest (optional)
- 1 tablespoon dark rum (optional)

## Instructions
1) Combine the butter and sugar. Beat at medium speed until creamy. 
2) At low speed, add the almond flour, alternating with egg until the ingredients are incorporated evenly. 
3) Add the flour and mix to combine. 
4) Add the vanilla extract, lemon zest (optional), and rum (optional) and mix until just combined. 
5) Use right away or transfer to a container and refrigerate until ready to use.
