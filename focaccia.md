# Focaccia 
- 75% Hydration plus olive oil if mixing olive oil into dough 
- 10% Starter
- 2% Salt

# Measurements 
### Dough
- 50 grams bread flour
- 50 grams all purpose flour
- 75 grams water
- 10 grams 100% hydration starter made with all purpose flour
- 2 grams salt

### Brine
- 50% Water
- 50% Oil
- Salt

#### Pan sizes:
- 9" x 13" Baking sheet 600 grams flour

## Procedure
1. Add water to mixing bowl.
2. Add starter and salt and mix into water.
3. Add flour and mix until all combined. 
4. Let dough rise overnight.
5. Put dough into baking pan and stretch to spread it across most of the pan. 
6. Allow to settle into pan and proof for 2 hours.
7. Coat top of dough with brine. 
8. Dimple dough with fingers.
9. Top with any additions. 
10. Bake at 450 until top is golden brown. Approximately 20 minutes.
