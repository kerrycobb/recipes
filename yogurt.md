# Instant Pot Yogurt
## Ingredients
- 1 gal. Whole Milk
- 1/4 cup Yogurt starter 

## Directions
1. Heat milk on stove top until it has reached 180&deg;F/82&deg;C.
2. Let milk cool to 110&deg;F/43&deg;C.
3. Mix in yogurt starter 
4. Incubate in Instant pot with yogurt function for 8 hours. 
5. Pour yogurt into strainer lined with cheese cloth and place atop a large bowl in the refrigerator for about 12 hours.
