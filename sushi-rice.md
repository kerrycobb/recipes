# Sushi Rice
Makes enough for 3 rolls

## Ingredients
- 1 cup Short Grain Rice (Koshihikari is ideal)
- 1.25 cups water
- 1/4 cup vinegar
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2x2 inch strip kombu

## Directions
1. Rinse rice.
2. Add 1.5 parts water for each 1 part rice.
3. Cook rice with kombu in rice cooker. Stir partway through to stop rice from burning on the bottom.
4. While rice is cooking, heat vinegar, sugar, and salt on stove with low heat until everything is dissolved and remove from heat.
5. As soon as rice is finished cooking, spread rice out in bottom of a large vessel and pour sushi vinegar over rice. Use rice paddle to help spread the vinegar out over the top of the rice as it is poured.
6. Mix the rice until there are no longer any clumps using a slicing motion with the rice paddle to avoid crushing the rice grains.
7. Cover with a damp towel.
