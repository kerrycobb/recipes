# Merengue
- 1 Egg
- 4 tablepsoons of sugar, granulated or powdered
- Tiny pinch of kosher salt

# Directions

1. Througout the process, use utensils that are completely dry and free of grease/oil. It is best to avoid plastic since it's harder to clean. 
2. Separate whites from eggs while eggs are still cold.
3. Allow egg whites to reach room temperature.
6. Add sugar to egge whites.
7. Beat at high speed until stiff peaks form. Approximately 5-10 minutes.
