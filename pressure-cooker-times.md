
# Pressure Cooker Times

Add 3 parts water for each part un-soaked beans. Add 1/2 teaspoon salt per cup
of beans.

Cook on high pressure with 15 minute natural release.

| Type                               | Soaked Cook Time | Dry Cook Time |
| ---------------------------------- | ---------------- | ------------- |
| Adzuki Beans                       |  6 min           | 20 min        |
| Black Beans                        |  8 min           | 25 min        |
| Cannellini Beans                   | 15 min           | 35 min        |
| Chickpeas (Garbanzo Bean)          | 15 min           | 42 min        |
| Kidney Beans                       | 15 min           | 40 min        |
| Lentils                            | n/a              |  4 min        |
| Lima Beans                         |  8 min           | 35 min        |
| Mung Beans                         | n/a              | 17 min        |
| Pinto Beans                        | 15 min           | 30 min        |
| Split Peas                         | n/a              | 10 min        |
| White Beans (Great Northern)       | 20 min           | 30 min        |
