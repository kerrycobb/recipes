Makes 3 waffles

# Ingredients
- 200 grams 100% sourdough starter
- 100 grams buttermilk
- 75 grams oil
- 1 large egg
- 50 grams corn starch
- 25 grams sugar
- 15 grams vanilla extract
- 1/2 teaspoon or 3 grams salt
- 1/2 teaspoon or 2.5 grams baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoonor 1.5 grams baking soda

# Directions
1. Mix all ingredients being careful not to over mix.
2. Let rest for 30 minutes
3. Pour 160 grams batter for each waffle into waffle iron and cook ~4 minutes.
4. Warm and recrisp in toaster oven.
