# Pecan Pie

## Ingredients
### Filling
- 1 cup cream or milk
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1/2 cup chopped pecans 
- Pinch of salt
- layer of nuts on top
### Merengue
- 2 Egg whites
- 2-3 Tablespoons powdered sugar



## Directions

1. Pie dough: 425&deg; for 12 min with baking bean, then, remove beans and cook 5 or 7 min until botton becomes brown.
2.  then set the oven to 350&deg;
