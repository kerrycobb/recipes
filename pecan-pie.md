# Pecan Pie

Modified from [Tasting History](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sIFlPc-TW94&t=237s)

## Ingredients
[Pie Crust](pie-crust.md)

### Filling
- 1 cup cream or milk
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 cup chopped pecans 
- Pinch of salt
- Layer of nuts for top (optional)
- Merengue from two egg whites (optional)

## Directions
1. Prepare a [Pie Crust](pie-crust.md).
2. Turn oven down to 350&deg;.
3. Whisk eggs in a bowl.
4. In a saucepan, mix cream/milk, sugar, eggs, flour, pecans, and salt.
6. Cook over low heat until thickened. About 10 minutes.
7. Pour filling into cooked pie crust and optionally place later of nuts on top.
8. Bake in oven for 30-35 minutes at 350&deg; or until there is a slight wobble in the center. 
9. Allow to cool.
10. Optionally spread or pipe merengue onto pie. Return to 350&deg; oven for 12 minutes or until browned.
