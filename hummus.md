# Hummus

[Source](https://www.seriouseats.com/recipes/2016/03/israeli-style-extra-smooth-hummus-recipe.html)

## Ingredients
- 1 1/2 cups/250 g dried chickpeas or 3 2/3 cups cooked chickpeas
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 6 1/2 cups/1.5 liters water
- 1 cup plus 2 tablespoons/270 g light tahini paste
- 4 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
- 4 cloves garlic, crushed
- 6 1/2 tablespoons/100 ml ice-cold water
- salt

## Directions
1. Place the chickpeas in a food processor and process until you get a stiff paste. Then, with the machine still running, add the tahini paste, lemon juice, garlic, and 1 1/2 teaspoons salt. Finally, slowly drizzle in the iced water and allow it to mix for about 5 minutes, until you get a very smooth and creamy paste.
