# Store Bought Puff Pastry

1. Thaw ~20 minutes.
2. Flour all surfaces of dough.
3. Roll out between two sheets of parchment paper.
4. Dock with lots of holes using fork.
5. Cook, sandwiched between two baking sheets at 430&def;F for 15 minutes.
6. Remove top baking sheet and cook for 5 more minutes or until brown.
