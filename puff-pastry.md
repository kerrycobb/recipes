# Store Bought Puff Pastry
When needing flat, thin pieces of puff pastry:
1. Thaw ~20 minutes.
2. Flour surface of dough on each side.
3. Roll out between two sheets of parchment paper, separating from each piece of parchment periodically.
4. Dock with lots of holes using fork.
5. Cut to desired shapes.
6. Cook, sandwiched between two baking sheets at 400&def;F for 25 minutes or until top surface is golden brown.
